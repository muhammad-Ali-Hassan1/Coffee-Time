OUR STORY

Our Journey

Our founder Mush Panjwani discovered his love for coffee culture while working in Hong Kong. During visits to Pakistan in 2016-17, he realized the lack of quality coffee spaces offering affordability, comfort, and modern café vibes.

Inspired by this gap, Mush returned to Pakistan to build a coffee brand that could redefine café culture — aiming to become the “Starbucks of Pakistan”.

Since launching our first outlet in 2018, Coffee Time has grown into Pakistan's largest coffee brand, with multiple locations, café models, a barista academy, online store, and loyalty app.

COFFEE TIME

Coffee Time was launched in August 2024 with a vision to become Pakistan’s most trusted and premium coffee brand.
