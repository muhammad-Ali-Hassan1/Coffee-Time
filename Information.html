WORKSPACE

Workspace at Coffee Time

This is only viable when customers respect and follow the guidelines:

1. Please place your order before connecting your laptop or phone.

2. Minimum spending is Rs. 500 + GST per person per visit (after discounts).

3. You may pack items for takeaway to meet minimum spend.

4. If you leave your seat, please take your belongings with you. Staff cannot hold items.

5. Smoking breaks are allowed only right outside the café.

6. Friends or colleagues joining you must place an order first.

7. For any clarification or feedback, please WhatsApp the number provided on the receipt.

Thank you for respecting our workspace culture.
